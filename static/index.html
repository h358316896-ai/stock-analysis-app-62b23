FAQ
Frequently asked questions
What markets does StockAI cover? +
A-Share (Shanghai & Shenzhen), Hong Kong (HKEX), and US markets (NYSE & NASDAQ). Plus global indices from Japan, Korea, India, Europe, commodities, and cryptocurrencies.
How does the AI analysis work? +
Our AI fetches real-time quotes, recent K-line data, and applies DeepSeek's large language model to generate multi-dimensional analysis reports covering technicals, fundamentals, valuation, news sentiment, sector comparison, and risk assessment.
Is the data real-time? +
Yes. Market data refreshes every 30 seconds via Tencent Finance and Eastmoney APIs. US market data is sourced from Yahoo Finance for delayed quotes (15-min delay per exchange requirements).
Can I save my analysis and track stocks? +
Absolutely. Create a free account to maintain a watchlist, set price alerts (above/below thresholds, % change triggers), and save all your AI analysis history for later review.
